## 🔍 Understanding UptimeRobot Status

### Status Colors:
- 🟢 **UP (Green)**: Service is responding correctly
- 🔴 **Down (Red)**: Service is not responding (sleeping or error)
- 🟡 **Preparing (Yellow)**: Initial check in progress

### Uptime Percentage:
- **0.01%**: Just started, need more checks
- **50-80%**: Some checks failed (service waking up)
- **95-100%**: Service is consistently awake ✅
