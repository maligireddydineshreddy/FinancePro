# 🗑️ Delete User - Step by Step in MongoDB Atlas

## 🎯 From Where You Are Now

You're on the **Project Overview** page. Here's how to delete a user:

---

## Step 1: Go to Browse Collections

1. In the **Clusters** section, click the **"Browse collections"** card
   - It has an icon that looks like stacked documents with a green plus sign
   - Or click the "Browse Collections" link in the left sidebar under "DATABASE"

---

## Step 2: Select Database and Collection

1. After clicking "Browse collections", you'll see your database
2. Click on database: **`financeApp`**
3. Click on collection: **`users`**

---

## Step 3: Find and Delete the User

1. You'll see a list of all users
2. Each user document will show their data (email, name, etc.)
3. **Find the user** you want to delete:
   - Look for their email or name
   - Click on the user document (row) to select it
4. **Delete the user:**
   - Click the **trash icon** (🗑️) at the top of the page
   - Or right-click on the document → Delete
   - Confirm the deletion

---

## ✅ Done!

The user has been deleted from the database.

---
